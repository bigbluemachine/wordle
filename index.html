Guess the word in six tries or fewer. Each guess must be a valid word. After you make a guess, each letter is given a color:

  • Green — in the correct location
  • Orange — in a different location
  • Grey — not in the word (but watch out for repeats!)

When all except one letter is correct, then letters that can go there will be highlighted on the keyboard.

Unlike the official Wordle game, words are randomly selected from the Scrabble word list. Hence some of the words may be... interesting.

After you finish a game, there will be a link to the dictionary entry for the word — go have a look!

Play a random word, or make a puzzle with a specific word by entering a seed. You can send any puzzle to a friend by giving them the link.

Have fun!
